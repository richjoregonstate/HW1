The three levels of Abstration
ADT - Abstract Data Type
	-Defines main characteristics of the container.
	-Specifies operations over the container
	-Interface - in a particular libary of containers
		-Defines how to use it
		-Behavior constraints
	-Implementation - in a particular libary
		-Specifies how it works
	-Basic ADT's
		-Bag, ordered bag
		-Stack, queue, Deque
		-Priority Queue
		-List
		-Hash
